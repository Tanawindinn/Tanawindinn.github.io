About us

Private Unitary Transport Enterprise "BELATEKAVTO" has been in the freight transportation market since 2008. Since 2016, it has been a full member of the Association of International Road Carriers "BAMAP" and since 2020, it has been a member of the Belarusian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (CCI). Since 2024, it has been an associate member of The Belarusian Association of International Forwarders. The company specializes in transport and forwarding services, as well as international freight transportation using its own fleet.
The company was awarded the Diploma of the National Competition "Entrepreneur of the Year" in the category "Efficient Business in the Service Sector" in 2016.
Since 2022, the company has been a multimodal service operator, actively contributing to the development of the West-East and North-South transport corridors in the interests of Belarusian exporters and importers. It specializes in comprehensive services to China, India, Pakistan, Turkey, the UAE, Egypt, and other long-distance countries.
The company is an accredited carrier in the ports of St. Petersburg and Novorossiysk.
The rapid growth of the enterprise has enabled the establishment of strong international partnerships, expansion of transportation geography, attraction of highly qualified personnel, and acquisition of extensive experience. In its activities, the company strives for continuous development and exploration of new areas in the transport services market.

Modern and Reliable Transport

We use only modern and reliable equipment from the European manufacturer VOLVO. Our fleet includes curtain-sided semi-trailers and container platforms with a load capacity of up to 30 tons.

GPS Monitoring

All vehicles are equipped with satellite GPS monitoring systems. At any moment, our clients can receive up-to-date information about the location of the transported cargo.

Competent Personnel

Our team consists of experienced drivers and highly qualified manager-specialists. This allows us to provide services that perfectly meet the requirements of our clients.
